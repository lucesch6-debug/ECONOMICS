=== Slide 21 ===
Round 4: The Green Market
21
Story:
A new government has been elected, which promised to cut taxes while staying on the sustainability journey.
Greenerland
replaces the carbon tax with a cap-and-trade system. Each firm receives 35 pollution rights, with each right covering 1 kg of CO
₂
. Firms can trade rights in bilateral exchanges. Technologies have not changed, with some firms polluting more than others.
Your task:
Decide how much to produce and whether to buy, sell, or hold pollution rights. Negotiate with another firm to maximize your profit.
Outcome:
Report your production, profit, and emissions, along with any trades
